Welcome to the Saito Network

Most blockchains force users to store data forever, and pay separate fees for infrastructure providers since all fees go to miners and stakers. Saito fixes both problems.

On Saito, applications pay only for the storage they need, while the network uses a new kind of mining that secures the blockchain with data-routing infrastructure. The result is lower fees and higher throughput than any other blockchain.
